# Tickets — Help & Resources

## What is Tickets?

Tickets is an add-on app for ORIGIN that provides a trouble ticket system. It helps you manage support requests, track issues, and handle customer service inquiries all within your workspace.

## How to Enable

1. Go to the **Marketplace** in your workspace sidebar
2. Find **Tickets** under the Apps category
3. Click **Install** to enable it

## Getting Started

Once enabled, you will see **Tickets** appear in the Apps section of your sidebar. Click on it to access the Tickets dashboard.

## Planned Features

- **Ticket Inbox** — View and triage incoming support requests
- **Status Workflow** — Track tickets through Open, In Progress, Resolved, and Closed statuses
- **Agent Assignment** — Assign tickets to team members for resolution
- **Notifications** — Receive email and in-app alerts when tickets are updated
- **Customer Portal** — Public-facing page for customers to submit and track tickets
